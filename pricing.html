Music Production


We believe music production is for everyone, whether you're a complete beginner experimenting with sounds for the first time, or a developing artist ready to shape your own tracks and identity. Our approach focuses on hands-on creativity, guiding students through beat-making, sound design, recording, arranging, and mixing using industry-standard software and equipment. Lessons are paced to suit individual learning styles, encouraging curiosity, confidence, and musical expression.

Students will have the opportunity to work on their own projects, develop technical understanding, and build skills that can support school music programs, personal artistry, or future professional pathways.

  • Children (Under 13): €20 per lesson / €70 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Students (13–18 or with Student ID): €25 per lesson / €90 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Adults: €35 per lesson / €120 for 4-lesson monthly bundle


Piano and Keyboard


The piano and keyboard are timeless instruments that opens the door to musical expression, creativity, and technical confidence. Our lessons support learners of all ages, whether they are discovering the keyboard for the first time or developing existing abilities. Students can explore classical pieces, popular music, improvisation, and music theory in a supportive and relaxed environment.

We place emphasis on good posture, hand technique, ear training, rhythm, and musical enjoyment, ensuring every lesson feels meaningful and encouraging. Progress is steady, positive, and tailored to personal goals.

  • Children (Under 13): €20 per lesson / €70 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Students (13–18 or with Student ID): €25 per lesson / €90 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Adults: €35 per lesson / €120 for 4-lesson monthly bundle


Drumming & Percussion


Drumming builds rhythm, coordination, and confidence — and it’s one of the most energizing and joyful ways to learn music. Our drumming and percussion lessons introduce students to stick technique, timing, groove patterns, hand independence, drum kit set-up, and musical expression. Whether playing along to favourite songs or developing technical routines, students learn in a structured yet encouraging environment.

Lessons are active, dynamic, and engaging, helping learners develop both physical skill and musical awareness. Great for beginners and experienced players alike.

  • Children (Under 13): €20 per lesson / €70 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Students (13–18 or with Student ID): €25 per lesson / €90 for 4-lesson monthly bundle
  • Adults: €35 per lesson / €120 for 4-lesson monthly bundle


Guitar


Whether you're drawn to acoustic strumming, classical fingerstyle, or electric guitar riffs, our guitar lessons help students develop a strong and expressive playing foundation. Lessons are tailored to each learner’s interests, covering chords, melody, rhythm, tablature and notation reading, tone, technique, and playing with confidence.

Students are encouraged to learn through the music they love, while also building the technique needed to progress smoothly and enjoyably. These lessons are suitable for complete beginners through to advancing players looking to refine their sound.
