Music Production Course

Turn your passion for sound into powerful, professional tracks! This hands-on course takes you inside the world of modern music production — from beat-making and recording to mixing and mastering. Learn to work with top industry software, craft your own unique sound, and bring your creative vision to life. Whether you dream of producing for artists, film, or gaming, this course gives you the skills and confidence to make it happen.

What You’ll Learn

  • Understanding DAWs (Logic, FL Studio, Ableton, etc)
  • Beat-making & MIDI programming
  • Recording vocals & instruments
  • Sound design & synth basics
  • Mixing essentials (EQ, compression, effects)
  • Mastering and exporting a final track

Course Outline

  • Lesson 1: Introduction to DAWs & Workflow
  • Lesson 2: Making Beats & Using MIDI
  • Lesson 3: Recording Techniques
  • Lesson 4: Editing & Arranging
  • Lesson 5: Mixing Fundamentals
  • Lesson 6: Mastering & Final Export

Drumming Course

Feel the rhythm and unleash your inner percussionist! Our drumming course is designed to get you playing powerful beats and dynamic grooves from day one. Learn essential techniques, stick control, timing, and how to play in a band or record your own tracks. Whether you’re into rock, jazz, funk, or pop — you’ll build the confidence and skill to command any stage or studio.

What You’ll Learn

  • Grip, posture & basic technique
  • Rock, pop & funk grooves
  • Drum notation basics
  • Drum fills & transitions
  • Coordination & independence
  • Playing along with real songs

Course Outline

  • Lesson 1: Technique & simple rhythms
  • Lesson 2: Essential grooves
  • Lesson 3: Fills & creativity
  • Lesson 4: Reading & counting
  • Lesson 5: Independence training
  • Lesson 6: Full-song performance

Piano & Keyboard Course

Unlock the language of music through the keys! This course combines creativity and technique, helping you master chords, melodies, and harmony while developing a strong ear for music. Learn to play your favorite songs, compose your own pieces, and explore both classical and contemporary styles — all with the guidance of passionate, professional instructors.

What You’ll Learn

  • Proper hand technique
  • Reading notation or chord sheets
  • Major & minor scales
  • Chord progressions
  • Melody-and-chords song playing
  • Using digital keyboard sounds

Course Outline

  • Lesson 1: Intro & finger exercises
  • Lesson 2: Reading music / chord basics
  • Lesson 3: Scales & patterns
  • Lesson 4: Chord progressions
  • Lesson 5: Melody & two-hand playing
  • Lesson 6: Complete song performance

Guitar Course

Turn up the volume and let your talent shine! Our guitar course covers everything from strumming your first chords to shredding solos and improvising with confidence. Explore acoustic and electric guitar techniques, music theory, and performance skills — all while learning the songs and styles you love. Perfect for beginners and seasoned players ready to take their sound to the next level.

What You’ll Learn

  • Basic chords & transitions
  • Strumming patterns and rhythm
  • Tabs & simple riffs
  • Fingerstyle basics
  • Pentatonic scale & intro to solos
  • Playing full songs

Course Outline

  • Lesson 1: Tuning & first chords
  • Lesson 2: Strumming patterns
  • Lesson 3: Tabs & riffs
  • Lesson 4: Chord progressions
  • Lesson 5: Scales & improvisation
  • Lesson 6: Play a complete song
